Our Story

Welcome to El Pacha Restaurant, where culinary excellence meets Tunisian hospitality in the heart of Sousse. Our restaurant features two distinct dining experiences - a formal restaurant-style room and a relaxed lounge-style area.

We are dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience with a perfect blend of traditional Tunisian flavors and contemporary culinary techniques. Our varied menu caters to all tastes, from fresh seafood to premium meats.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal, El Pacha Restaurant offers the perfect ambiance for every dining experience.
